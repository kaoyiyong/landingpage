ABOUT US

Being the voices of the strays for the love they deserve.

Pawfinder is an online database of animals who need homes. Our mission is to increase the public's awareness of the availability of high-quality adoptable pets, elevate the status of pets to that of family member and allow cat lovers to look for the cat that best matches their needs with the help of the Internet.
